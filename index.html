Soft Skills

University extension project

Mandacaru Aerodesign it’s a university extension project with more than twenty engineering students. The objective it’s to anually build an airplane following SAE regulation, to compete at national level against other universities.

Working three years in this project I desenvolved:

Team spirit - Working with more than twenty other students in a collaborative enviroment with a competitive objective. I had to learn to lead and to be leaded, to hear and to be heard.

Creativity - I had to solve several problems in the project, some of them were not really solvable with theory, so I had to solve them in a creative way.

Analytic thinking - Before solving the problems, I had to indentify and define them, extracting usefull variables tha would help me see the problem clearly. And then I could solve the problem using what I learned in engineering.

Resiliency - Some problems with the airplane were really unpredictable, so we had to be always ready to face hard times and difficulties. And to be competitive we had to face, adapt and overcome any problem. In 2018 every single plane flied with ease, in 2019 every single plane crashed out of nowhere.
